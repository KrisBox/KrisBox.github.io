The Diamond

About The Diamond

The Diamond is a brand new 81 Million pound building opened by The University of Sheffield in September 2015. It boasts the most update technology whilst giving a futuristic warm feel to students and staff alike.

Who uses it?

The Diamond is opened to all staff and students 24/7. Mainly used by engineering students, it provides the lecture theatres needed and study areas for catch up. The Diamond contains 9 lecture theatres, one containing 400 seats and 8 smaller ones in the basement and upper levels.

What makes it so good?

Boasting a clean room, virtual reality suite, pilot plant and many other laboratories for engineering students, and computer labs for Computer Scientists, the Diamond has the facilities to cater for any seminar or tutorial.
