My Birthday

Date: 28th November 2015

Saturday 28th November was my 19th birthday. Receiving all my cards and presents in the post in the week leading up to the day, I was getting more and more excited to spend my first birthday in Sheffield. Having my sister come and see me for the day and night truly made my birthday more enjoyable with spending the day at the christmas markets and winning 8-2 in the football. To top a good day off, we went to town during the night with the flat and my sister's friend. Genuinely a birthday to remember.

My First Week

Date: 4th October 2015

This week was my first week as a Computer Scientist. It was a very interesting week, having introductions to what all my modules will be teaching throughout the year. I am very excited to be getting into this degree and to develop my skills in the field. Also this week, we met our tutors and was given free pizza and beer! A wonderful welcoming gift I must admit. I enjoy the busy timetable with the variety of areas being covered. Much preferred to some of my flat mates who only have a handful of hours per week. Also, we won the Lego Robot football competition!

Moving to the University!

Date: 21st September 2015

Today I moved into my flat. After a tedious journey from Burnley through Snakes Pass, and many trips up and down the stairs to move my stuff around, we are finally here and moving in! Meeting all my new flat mates is a real buzz, very excited to get to know them better. It was sad to see the parents leave but I am very excited to start the course next week.
